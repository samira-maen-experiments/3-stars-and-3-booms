How to Play

The goal is to move all 3 Stars and 3 Bombs from the right bank to the left bank.

  • Capacity: The boat can carry a maximum of 2 characters.
  • Operation: The boat needs at least 1 character to cross.
  • Safety Rule: Do not leave the Stars outnumbered by Bombs on either bank! If there are more Bombs than Stars on a side (and there is at least one Star on that side), the Bombs will detonate, and it's game over!
  • How to move characters:
    • Click a character on the same side as the boat to load them.
    • Click a character inside the boat to unload them.
    • Click the GO button to sail the boat to the opposite bank.
